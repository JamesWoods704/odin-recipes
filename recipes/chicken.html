Lemon Pepper Chicken

Description

The perfect dish for any ocasion.Lemon pepper chicken is full of flavour and citris, and is a great way to prepare chicken. this recipe is a go to.

Ingredients
  • Cleaned Chicken Breast
  • Lemons
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Chicken Stock
Steps
  1. Mince garlic and Season chicken breast's
  2. Heat up a pan and cook your chicken breasts
  3. Set aside chicken when cooked and put some butter in the pan with the garlic and cook until fragrent
  4. Deglaze pan with chicken stock then put your chicken back in with sliced lemons
  5. Season to taste and your good to go!
