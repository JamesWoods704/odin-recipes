Tuscan Salmon

Description

The perfect salmon dish packed with flavour.Creamy sauce with tomatoes and spinich with a perfectly cooked salmon.What better way to impress your guests or treat yourself.

Ingredients
  • Salmon
  • Roasted tomatoes
  • Spinich
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesean
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps
  1. Get a pan hot and put your salmon in along with some oil
  2. Cook for 3 minutes then flip and cook another 3 minutes
  3. Set aside salmon and put your tomatoes and garlic in the pan with some more oil
  4. Cook until garlic is fragrent then pour in heavy cream and some parmesean
  5. Let reduce then put in spinich and seasonings
  6. Plate salmon and pour cream sauce over
