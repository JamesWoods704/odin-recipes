Cajun pasta

Description

This cajun pasta is the perfect dish for those who like a rich creamy pasta with a bit of a kick,full of protein and flavour this dish is perfect for any pasta lover

Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Sasauge
  • Heavy Cream
  • Paemasean Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Peppers and onions
  • Chicken Breast
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Prepare your proteins and heat up a pans
  2. Grill sausage and chicken or cook it in a pan then set aside
  3. In your pan put your crushed garlic,peppers,onions, and butter and cook until fragrent
  4. Once fragrent pour heavy cream and deglaze pan
  5. Finally add pasta with some parmesean and toss through
  6. Plate everything and garnish with cheese
